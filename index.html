Simon HW

Engineering Physics Student at UBC

Hello! I’m Simon Hathout Willard, an engineering student who is interested in HVAC technologies, Nuclear Power, and implementing proven technologies to build us towards a green future. At UBC, I’ve been building a sensor system with the Agoponics Design Team to improve plant growth.

This summer, I’ve been doing a lot of projects at Northforge, a makerspace in Winnipeg. So far, I’ve been trained on woodworking tools, as well as laser cutters.

In my free time, I enjoy cross stitching, orienteering, and have recently started playing piano.
